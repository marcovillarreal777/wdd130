Music

This is meant to be a section for me to showcase every piece of music that I have out right now. I am going to link the song/album and provide any related pictures. I also am going to write the story behind every song that I have out. I have experiences making every piece of music that I have out and I want the people that like the songs and support my music to know what really went into it.
——————————
For example my album ‘Nightfishing’:
For this album it really only came out around 5-6 months after I first started learning music production. At the time I had a decent amount of melodies that I had made trying to replicate the type of chill video game music that I like. I have been mainly inspired by the Donkey Kong soundtracks and other music like that. I took a short break from production on a trip to New York City to visit my older brother. After this break I came back and relentlessly worked on break core, drum and bass, and jungle music, when that was not really what I was making previous to that. During this time I didn’t make a single new melody. I found songs I liked around the internet, sampled them and added what I felt would sound good. If it didn’t immediately sound good I gave up on the sample and moved on to another one. There were points I was making 4 demos a night and I have upwards of 40 throwaways from this 15 song project. When I tell people that they tend to not really believe me but its true. The catch however is that many of them were only worked on for a short time before I realized it wouldn’t work and moved on. Toward the end of the project I went back and sampled many of my own melodies and put 5 of those on the project. I would add different mixes of delays, reverb, flangers, filters, and whatever else to make the melodies of what I sampled more psychedelic and entrancing. I feel like the development of this record was very quick as I believe it only took about 2 months. I moved to college when almost done with the project and simply tweaked all of the songs until I liked them and uploaded for release a few weeks into my freshman fall semester. Overall I think that the record is pretty good for a drum and bass record, and I tried my best at the time but I know that in all aspects I could do better now.
